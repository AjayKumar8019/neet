Why Do Students Underperform on the NEET UG Exam?

Even the most prepared students sometimes fail to perform their best on exam day due to poor optimization of Brain Energy Reserves (BER).

Brain energy reserves is critical factor for mental readiness and availability in the examination hall. This is often due to factors beyond academic preparation, such as:

  1. High Stress and Anxiety: Intense pressure leads to mental blocks and hinders clarity.
  2. Inadequate Time Management: Struggling to allocate time effectively during the exam.
  3. Overthinking and Self-Doubt: A lack of confidence can derail even the best-prepared minds.
  4. Poor Focus and Concentration: Distractions or fatigue can lower efficiency.

Neglecting Mental Readiness: Focusing only on academics without conditioning the mind for high-stakes performance.
