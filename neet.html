The Medical Counselling Committee (MCC) conducts the centralized NEET-UG counseling for 15% All India Quota (AIQ) seats, Deemed Universities, Central Universities, and ESIC/AFMC institutes.

Whether you're aiming for AIIMS, JIPMER, or top Deemed universities, understanding the MCC counseling process is crucial to making the right choices and avoiding costly mistakes.

What is MCC Counselling?

MCC NEET-UG Counselling is an online process that allows qualified NEET students to:

  • Apply for All India Quota (AIQ) 15% seats in Government Medical Colleges
  • Get admission in AIIMS, JIPMER, ESIC, AFMC & Deemed Universities
  • Participate in multiple rounds including mop-up and stray vacancy rounds

Key Features:

  • Entirely online registration, choice filling & seat allotment
  • Open to all NEET-UG qualified students across India
  • Offers some of India's top MBBS and BDS colleges
  • Transparent & merit-based seat allocation
